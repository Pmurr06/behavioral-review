How Recipients Are Selected

Student Writer of the Month recognizes an undergraduate author whose published work demonstrates exceptional originality, analytical rigor, and interdisciplinary scholarship. Recipients are selected by The Behavioral Review editorial board based on the quality, originality, and impact of their published work.

Originality

Creative thinking and novel contribution.

Research Quality

Strong evidence, methodology, and scholarship.

Analytical Depth

Clear reasoning and meaningful insight.

Writing Excellence

Professional organization, clarity, and academic communication.
