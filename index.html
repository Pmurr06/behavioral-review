WHY PUBLISH WITH THE BEHAVIORAL REVIEW?

  • Publish under your own name
  • Transform coursework into portfolio pieces
  • Strengthen graduate school and job applications
  • Gain experience with an editorial review process
  • Receive publication credit for original academic work
  • Build a permanent online record of your research

Many submissions begin as class papers, research projects, case studies, literature reviews, or policy analyses. A strong paper you've already written may be eligible for publication with revision.

WHO SHOULD SUBMIT?

Undergraduate students researching human behavior, criminal justice, decision-making, public policy, or social science are encouraged to submit.
