WHY PUBLISH WITH THE BEHAVIORAL REVIEW?

  • Publish under your own name
  • Transform coursework into professional portfolio pieces
  • Strengthen graduate school, law school, internship, and job applications
  • Gain experience working with an editorial review process
  • Receive publication credit for original academic work
  • Join a growing network of student researchers and contributors
  • Build a permanent archive of published work accessible online

Many submissions begin as class papers, research projects, case studies, literature reviews, policy analyses, or editorial essays. If you have already written a strong paper for a course, it may be eligible for publication.

Our Editorial Review Process

1

Submission Received by Managing Editor

Your manuscript is received, logged, and reviewed for completeness.

2

Manuscript Logged and Anonymized

The Managing Editor logs your submission and removes all author identifying information.

3

Section Editor Review

The anonymized paper is sent to the appropriate Section Editor for detailed editorial feedback.

4

Editorial Feedback and Revision

You receive feedback and have the opportunity to revise and resubmit your manuscript.

5

Copy & Layout Editor Review

Our Copy & Layout Editor reviews formatting, grammar, citation consistency, AI-assisted content screening, and publication readiness.

6

Managing Editor Final Approval

The Managing Editor makes the final approval decision and determines publication readiness.

7

Publication

Your article is published online, added to our archive, and featured in Recent Articles.
