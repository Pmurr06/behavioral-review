For Authors

What We Publish

Original Research

Empirical and analytical studies that present a clear research question, method, and evidence-based findings. Submissions should contribute meaningful insight to behavioral and social science conversations.

Literature Reviews

Structured reviews that synthesize scholarship, identify patterns in the literature, and explain gaps for future research. Strong submissions offer interpretation, not just summary.

Case Analyses

Focused examinations of real-world events, policies, or decisions using behavioral or social science frameworks. Case analyses should connect evidence to a clear argument.

Commentary & Policy Analysis

Argument-driven pieces that evaluate policy questions, institutional decisions, or current debates. Effective commentary is evidence-based, balanced, and academically grounded.

Submission Requirements

Eligible Authors

Submissions are accepted from currently enrolled undergraduate authors, including individual and co-authored work.

Manuscript Length

1,500–5,000 words (including references)

Original Work

Manuscripts must be your own original work and must properly acknowledge all sources and referenced ideas.

APA Citation Style

Use APA 7 for citations and references throughout your manuscript.

Professional Academic Writing

Write in a clear, formal academic tone with strong organization, careful proofreading, and evidence-based analysis.

Editorial Review Process

After submission, your manuscript moves through these editorial steps, with most decisions issued within 2–4 weeks.

1

Initial Check

The Managing Editor confirms your submission is complete and within scope.

2

Blind Preparation

Identifying details are removed so the manuscript can be reviewed anonymously.

3

Section Review

A Section Editor evaluates your manuscript and provides editorial feedback.

4

Revision Stage

You receive feedback and may revise and resubmit for further review.

5

Copy & Layout Review

The copy and layout review checks clarity, formatting, and citation consistency.

6

Final Decision

The Managing Editor issues the final publication decision.

7

Publication

Accepted manuscripts are published online and added to the journal archive.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I submit a revised class paper?

Yes—revised class papers are welcome when they are substantially improved, clearly argued, and fully cited.

Can multiple authors submit together?

Yes—co-authored manuscripts are accepted when all contributors are identified at submission.

Can I submit to another journal while under review?

No—simultaneous submissions are not permitted while your manuscript is under consideration. You may submit elsewhere only after withdrawal or a final decision.
