Frequently asked questions

FAQs

How do I submit my work?

Email your manuscript to contact@thebehavioralreview.com with the subject line “Manuscript Submission — [Your Title].” Prepare manuscripts using APA 7 guidelines, and visit the For Authors page for full requirements.

Can I submit a revised class paper?

Yes, as long as it has been significantly revised, expanded, and properly cited.

How long does the review process take?

Typically 2–4 weeks, depending on current workload. The editorial team will keep you updated throughout the process.

Do I keep the rights to my published work?

Yes, you retain all rights to your work. The Behavioral Review simply has permission to publish and archive it.

What if my submission is not accepted?

We provide feedback on all submissions, and many authors revise and resubmit.
