Frequently Asked Questions

How do I submit my work?

Visit our For Authors page for complete submission guidelines. Official submission instructions will be coming soon.

Can I submit a revised class paper?

Yes! As long as it's been significantly revised, expanded, and properly cited, a class paper can become a published piece. Learn more on our For Authors page.

How long does the review process take?

Typically 2–4 weeks, depending on our current workload. We'll keep you updated throughout the process.

Do I keep the rights to my published work?

Yes, you retain all rights to your published work. The Behavioral Review simply has permission to publish it and make it available in our archive.

Can I be a Professor Ambassador?

We're looking for faculty who want to support undergraduate publication and mentor student researchers. Contact us to learn more about this opportunity.

What should I do if my submission is rejected?

We provide feedback on all submissions. Many authors revise and resubmit—we encourage you to use the feedback to strengthen your work and try again.
