Frequently Asked Questions

How do I submit my work?

Email your manuscript to contact@thebehavioralreview.com with the subject line "Manuscript Submission — [Your Title]". See the For Authors page for formatting requirements.

Can I submit a revised class paper?

Yes, as long as it has been significantly revised, expanded, and properly cited. Learn more on our For Authors page.

How long does the review process take?

Typically 2–4 weeks, depending on our current workload. We'll keep you updated throughout the process.

Do I keep the rights to my published work?

Yes, you retain all rights to your work. The Behavioral Review simply has permission to publish and archive it.

What if my submission is not accepted?

We provide feedback on all submissions. Many authors revise and resubmit — we encourage it.
