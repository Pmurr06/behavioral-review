Our Editorial Approach

Intellectual Integrity

We hold all submissions to clear standards for evidence-based reasoning and original thinking.

Author Support

We provide constructive feedback to help contributors develop their work, not just accept or reject it.

Consistent Process

Our editorial process follows a clear, transparent workflow from submission through publication.

Open to All Undergraduates

We welcome submissions from students at any institution, regardless of prior publication experience.
