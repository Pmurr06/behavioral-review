Editorial Team

Our Editors

Paulo Murray

Founder & Managing Editor

U.S. Marine | Psychology Student at Penn State World Campus

Paulo Murray is the founder and Managing Editor of The Behavioral Review. He is an active-duty U.S. Marine and psychology student pursuing a bachelor's degree. He founded The Behavioral Review to provide undergraduate students with opportunities to publish behavioral analysis, criminal case commentary, and scholarly work while building professional portfolios.

Nathan Isbell

Copy & Layout Editor

U.S. Marine | Writing Student at UMGC

Nathan Isbell is the Copy & Layout Editor for The Behavioral Review. He is an active-duty U.S. Marine, Intelligence Specialist, and writing student pursuing a bachelor's degree. He oversees article formatting, publication layout, citation consistency, editorial presentation, plagiarism screening, AI-assisted content detection, and publication readiness.
